ZeroRunWay Drone Strategy Project

Consulting-style market analysis conducted as part of a study abroad program in Munich, Germany.

This project evaluates the adoption of drone technology for maritime search and rescue (SAR), focusing on offshore energy platforms.

Key components of the analysis:
- Offshore SAR market landscape
- Competitive analysis of existing drone providers
- Market entry strategy for Denmark and Norway
- Financial projections and commercialization roadmap
